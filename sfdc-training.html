Connect to Salesforce

Enable the plugin, authorize, and verify the connection. Takes about 3 minutes.

The Salesforce plugin is already available in your Claude account under the Pelago section.

  1. Open the Claude desktop app
  2. Go to Settings → Plugins
  3. Under the Pelago section, find the Salesforce plugin
  4. Make sure it's enabled
That's it! No files to download — the plugin is managed by your admin.

Before connecting, make sure you're logged into Salesforce in your browser.

  1. Log out of any other Salesforce orgs (staging, sandbox, etc.)
  2. Go to your production Salesforce login page
  3. Log in with your credentials
  4. Keep this tab open — Claude will use this session to authenticate
Important: Only be logged into one Salesforce org at a time, or the connection can go to the wrong place.

Start a new Cowork session and ask Claude anything about Salesforce. It will prompt you to authorize the connection.

Who am I in Salesforce?
  1. A browser tab will open with a Salesforce authorization screen
  2. Review the permissions and click Allow
  3. The tab will close — go back to Claude
  4. Claude should return your name and Salesforce profile info
If you see your name, you're connected! You only need to do this once.

Learn the Basics

Try core queries and understand what Claude can (and can't) do with Salesforce.

Ask Claude to pull account data. Use plain English — no need to know SOQL or Salesforce query syntax.

Show me 10 accounts in Salesforce
Find the account for [type a real client name]

Claude will return a formatted table with account details. Try swapping in different account names or filters.

Find people at specific accounts or search across all contacts.

Who are the contacts at [account name]?
Find the contact info for [person's name]

Claude returns names, emails, phone numbers, titles, and account associations.

View your pipeline, filter by stage, or check what's closing soon.

What are our open opportunities?
Which opportunities are closing this quarter?
Show me our top 5 deals by Sellable Eligible Lives

Client Profiles contain program details, billing, eligibility, and marketing info for each client.

Show me the client profile for [client name]
What programs does [client name] have? Show me Tobacco, Alcohol, Opioid, and Cannabis
What are the billing details for [client name]?

Claude can read data and update existing records. But there are hard limits:

Creating new records is blocked. If you ask Claude to create a new contact, account, or any other record, it will refuse. Create records directly in Salesforce.
Deleting records is not available. Claude has no delete capability through this integration.
Updates require your confirmation. Claude will always show you what it's about to change and ask for a "yes" before making any update.

Try it — ask Claude to create something and watch it refuse:

Create a new contact named Test Person at Test Company

Client Account Overview

Get a full picture of a client before a meeting or QBR

▶
Prompt
Give me a complete overview of [client name]. Include their account details, all contacts and their roles, open opportunities, client profile info (programs, billing, eligibility, marketing tier), and any recent activity. Format it as a briefing doc I can review before a meeting.
What it does

Pulls from Accounts, Contacts, Opportunities, and Client Profile to build a single briefing document. Great for QBR prep or onboarding a new CSM to an account.

📈

Current IR and Pacing

Check invoiced revenue pacing for an account

▶
Prompt
Show me the invoiced revenue pacing for [client name]. Include current IR, pacing targets, and how they're trending against plan.
What it does

Pulls invoiced revenue and pacing data from the account to show how a client is tracking. Great for QBRs and internal check-ins.

▶

Marketing Tier Report

See which clients are in each marketing tier

▶
Prompt
Show me all client profiles grouped by marketing tier. For each tier, list the client name, comms cadence, and whether they're incentive eligible.
What it does

Queries Client Profile to pull Marketing_Tier__c, Comms_Cadence__c, and Incentive_Eligible__c across all clients, grouped for easy review.

Time in Stage Analysis

▶
Prompt
Show me all open opportunities with their current stage, how long they've been in that stage, and their last modified date. Flag any that have been in the same stage for more than 30 days.

Stale Deal Finder

▶
Prompt

What it does
